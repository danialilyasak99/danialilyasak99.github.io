About Us

The Blue Oasis Hotel began its operation in the year of 2014. The building has 15 floors with 2 underground parking lots, and it accommodates 480 rooms and a swimming pool. The Blue Oasis Hotel holds the concept of the combination of the modern era and contemporary with pockets of green spaces to add the feeling to be more at home and relax, enjoying the view and sun. There is also a traditional concept that will show the characteristics of Malay that will attract the guest and they will find it calmer and we can also show our tradition to foreigners.

The interior elements that they show are modern and contemporary concepts while the external design is more to the traditional characteristics to make the hotel have a ‘Comfort’ accommodation. The success of the Blue Oasis Hotel went beyond the expectations of developers and the management. It can be said that guests would have experienced a very impressionable and enjoyable stay during their time at the Blue Oasis Hotel.

Here in The Blue Oasis Hotel, we strive to provide our beloved hotel guests with the best and relaxing experience through sincere hospitality. We also promote an elegant and relaxing destination for guests in a friendly and harmony environment. After five years of operation, The Blue Oasis Hotel managed to get the recognition by getting the four-star hotel title by providing the guest with excellent cuisine, comfortable and larger rooms.

WAYV Technologies

WAYV Technologies is a Malaysian-based systems development company based in Petaling Jaya. WAYV Technologies was founded in 2010 as a company that aims to help businesses define the processes and systems to conduct their daily operations in a more effective manner. Our team is comprised of software developers and systems analysts from all paths of life to help aid clients of WAYV Technologies. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been hired by 20 clients comprised of well-known companies throughout Malaysia.
